My Recipe for Cheese Omlette

Ingredients

For cheesy omlette

  • eggs
  • Cheese
  • onion
  • cilantro
  • salt
  • pepper
  • tomatoes
  • oil

Instructions

  1. take a pan and add 2tbs oil
  2. add onions and tomatoes and saute till cooked
  3. add the dry ingredients
  4. pour the egg
  5. add shredded cheese on top of it
  6. close the lid and cook
  7. add cilantro and serve
